Themes

Participants can develop apps around any one of the following themes:

  1. Personal productivity
  2. Wellness
  3. Communications (involving voice, text or speech)

Teams

Teams can be formed before the hackathon, or at the hackathon itself. We encourage a mix of skills in each team: design, business development and engineering. Maximum team size is four members.

Judging criteria

Apps will be judged – on a scale of 1-10 – according to the following criteria:

  • Innovation
  • Adherence to design standards
  • Potential to generate revenue
  • Presentation skill
  • Judges' evaluation

Bonus points will be awarded to those teams who integrate Sony's / Amazon's / Intel's APIs/tools/services.
