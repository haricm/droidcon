Droidcon is India's largest Android developer conference, and is part of the world wide series of conferences that happens in London, Paris, Berlin, Netherlands, Tunis, Ankara and Brussels. The first edition of Droidcon India was at Bangalore in Nov 2011. The second edition in Nov 2012 was featured General & Specialized Topics, Native + HTML5 and App Demos. The 2013 edition featured dedicated tracks for deep dives into Systems, UX, Gaming, Business and App Demos.
